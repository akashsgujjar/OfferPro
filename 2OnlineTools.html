Health & Community Services
Health and Community Services is the industry in which theoretical knowledge regarding pure science and other parts of the STEM field come together to be applied to practical and clinical practices to maintain and improve the health of living creatures. Careers in this field are Social Worker,Nurse, or a Physician


Hospitality & Tourism
Hospitality and tourism is a constantly expanding industry that focuses on prioritizing the needs of other in a professional enviornment. A career in Hospitality and Tourism could mean a job as a Hotel Staff Member, Event Planner, or Catering Expert.


Office & Business Services
Office & Business Services is a large industry across the globe. It encompasses both Information Technology (Computer Science), and Business Development. Jobs available in this industry are in Human Resources, Security Services, or the Software Development.


Trade & Primary Industry
Trade & Primary Industry is the sector focused on the retrival of raw materials and supplies from the Earth's surface and below. Jobs in this industry focus on Mining, Forestry, and LoggingThese careers are sometimes viewed as less stable as they fluctuate greatly and can be affected by many factors.


Teaching Services
Teaching Services is one of the most fundemantly important career clusters. Teaching Services focuses on all aspects of education from School Teacher to Superintendent. Educating the younger generations is an essential aspect of personal and socitatl growth.


Retail
Retail is an industry that has a constant stream of jobs and there are always positions looking to be filled. A career in retail could be a Sales Representative, Cashier , or a Store Manager


Arts
The Arts are not a very traditional industry, but over the past few years, the demand for creative individuals has only risen. A career in this industry could be as a Painter, Music Artist , or even a Camera Operator
